UI / UX Guidelines (ui_guidelines.txt)

- Required field errors must be shown in red and have an icon.
- The "Submit Booking" button must be disabled until all required fields are valid and the T&C checkbox is checked.
- Inline validation is immediate (on blur) and summary validation also shown on submit attempt.
- Booking confirmation banner:
   - Text: "Booking Confirmed!"
   - Style: green background, accessible contrast, appears at top of page upon success.
- Seat map:
   - Available seats: clickable buttons with aria-label "Seat Row X Seat Y available".
   - Selected seat: highlighted, with aria-pressed=true.
- Mobile behavior:
   - Collapsible seat map, form stacks vertically.
- Error notifications:
   - Use inline messages near controls and a toast summary at the top.
